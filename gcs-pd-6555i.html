Overview

Fast and reliable 55 ppm B&W A4 printing, smart document capturing and handling, high quality in-house finishing capabilities, and trusted security.

Feature

  • A3 Multifunction B&W Print, copy, scan and send functionality
  • Print Speed 55 ppm (A4), 32 ppm (A3)
  • Double-sided Scanning BW: 240 ipm, color: 220 ipm (300dpi)
  • Print Resolution 1,200 dpi x 1,200 dpi
  • 25.6 cm (10.1 inch) TFT LCD WSVGA color Touch panel
  • UFRII and PCL6 printing

Detailed Features

Rapid printing results
Fast and reliable black and white printing, for high-volume business printing. Easy to use with a smartphone like touch screen, and powerful in-house finishing capabilities, with impressive results. Security features to help keep company data safe, and monitoring capabilities for easy management of devices.
